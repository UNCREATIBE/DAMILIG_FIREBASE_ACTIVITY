About Aventurine

He who was once a beloved child in the Desert must leave his entirety behind, left to be buried under the sands of time. His family, his people, his entire race... even his own name. Yet even as the only confirmed survivor, Aventurine, as the beloved and blssed child of their Mother Goddess survives with his unwavering luck even in the most hazardous moments. Now he sits on his throne of gold, working for the IPC as one of it's 10 cornerstones. Now given and being able to take all that he wants and doesn't want... just what is it that keeps the man working so...- no, too recklessly?
